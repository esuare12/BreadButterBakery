About Us

Handcrafted With Passion

Welcome to Bread + Butter Bakery, where our passion is baking. For us, baking isn't just a job; it's our craft. We are dedicated to the art of creating exceptional bread and pastries that bring joy to every table. We start with a commitment to time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients we can find. Our bread wall is a showcase of our devotion . From our airy, crusty sourdough to our flavorful focaccia and classic sandwich loaves, every item is made from scratch, in-house, and in small batches . We're constantly experimenting in the kitchen, dreaming up new flavors for our pastry case, from ambitious cupcakes to savory puff pastries . What drives us every day is the simple joy of seeing our community enjoy something we've poured our hearts into. Whether it's your weekly loaf or a special treat, we are proud to share our craft with you.

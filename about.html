About Us

Handcrafted With Passion

Welcome to Bread + Butter Bakery, where our passion is pastry. For us, baking isn't just a job; it's our craft. We are dedicated to the art of creating exceptional pastries that bring joy to every table. We start with a commitment to time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients we can find. Our pastry case is a showcase of our devotion. From our flaky, buttery croissants to our delicate macarons and classic cheese danishes, every item is made from scratch, in-house, and in small batches. We're constantly experimenting in the kitchen, dreaming up new flavors, from decadent tiramisu to creative seasonal treats. What drives us every day is the simple joy of seeing our community enjoy something we've poured our hearts into, and doing so with a deep commitment to reducing waste, from mindful batch planning to giving every ingredient its full purpose. Whether it's your morning croissant or a special dessert, we are proud to share our craft with you.
